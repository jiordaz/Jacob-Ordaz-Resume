  1. Classical Music
    • Johann Bach, Wolfgang Mozart, Ludwig van Beethoven, and Pyotr Tchaikovsky are among the most renowned classical composers.
    • Classical music has had a profound impact on various genres, including film music, jazz, and contemporary popular music.
    • Classical music is a global phenomenon, with composers, performers, and enthusiasts from diverse cultural backgrounds contributing to its evolution.
  2. Benefits
    • Listening to calming classical pieces can help lower cortisol levels and reduce overall stress.
    • The soothing and calming nature of classical music can contribute to better sleep quality.
    • Listening to classical music enhances cognitive functions such as memory and problem-solving skills.
